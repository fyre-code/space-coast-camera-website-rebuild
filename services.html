Camera & Lens Rentals

Brevard County's only local camera and lens rental shop. Whether you need a mirrorless body for a weekend shoot, a fast prime for an event, or a full kit for a commercial production, we have what you need at a fraction of the cost of ownership.

We offer daily and weekly rental rates on a curated selection of cameras, lenses, and accessories. All gear is inspected and cleaned before every rental, so you can focus on the shot — not the equipment.

Not sure what to rent? Call us and we'll help you figure out the right combination based on what you're shooting.

Sensor & Lens Cleaning

Dust on your sensor or smudges on your glass can quietly ruin photos for weeks before you notice. We offer professional sensor cleaning and lens cleaning services to get your gear back to factory-fresh condition.

Our cleaning process is careful and thorough — we use professional-grade tools and techniques to remove debris without damaging your sensor or coatings. If you're seeing spots in your images or softness that wasn't there before, it's time for a cleaning.

Contact us to schedule a drop-off and we'll have you sorted quickly.

Camera Troubleshooting

Gear acting up? Error messages, autofocus problems, exposure issues, or something else entirely? Bring it in. We'll take a look and help you figure out what's going on.

With over 48 years of imaging experience, Joe has seen just about every camera problem there is. Sometimes the fix is a simple menu setting or firmware update. Sometimes it's more involved. Either way, we'll give you an honest assessment and point you in the right direction.

We don't offer in-house repairs, but we can help diagnose the problem and tell you what your options are — without the guesswork.

Gear Sales

Looking to buy instead of rent? We periodically carry select camera and lens gear for sale — both new and pre-owned. We also purchase gear and offer trades if you're looking to upgrade or downsize your kit.

Our inventory changes, so the best approach is to call or text first to find out what's currently available. If we don't have what you need, we can often point you in the right direction.

Whether you're buying, selling, or trading, we'll give you a fair and honest deal — no pressure, no games.
