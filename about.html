About Space Coast Camera

Who We Are

Space Coast Camera & Lens Rental is Brevard County's only locally-owned camera and lens shop. We serve photographers and videographers all across Florida's Space Coast — from Cape Canaveral to Palm Bay — with high-quality gear available for daily or weekly rental.

Whether you're chasing a rocket launch, shooting a family portrait session, filming a commercial, or testing a lens before you buy, we have the equipment and the expertise to help you nail the shot. We believe great photography shouldn't require owning a $10,000 kit.

We also offer sensor cleaning, lens cleaning, camera troubleshooting, and beginner-to-advanced photography classes. This is more than a camera shop — it's a resource for the Space Coast creative community.

Meet The Owner

Joe Mitchell III

Joe Mitchell III is a Brevard County trial attorney and lifelong photography enthusiast with over 48 years of imaging experience. Joe founded Space Coast Camera & Lens Rental to make high-quality photographic equipment accessible to the creative community on Florida's Space Coast.

His background in law — specifically personal injury and trial work — has given him a deep appreciation for visual storytelling. The same precision and attention to detail he brings to the courtroom is woven into every aspect of Space Coast Camera.

Joe is hands-on at the shop and personally leads many of the photography classes offered on the calendar. When you visit, you may just find yourself getting advice straight from the owner.
